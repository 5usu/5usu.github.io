About Me

Hello! I'm a 20 right now, pursuing my bachelor's degree from Mumbai VJTI. My world revolves around the fascinating realms of Robotics and Machine Learning, where I spend countless hours exploring how intelligent systems can reshape our future.

Beyond the world of algorithms and circuits, I'm an avid reader who finds solace in books ranging from technical literature to philosophy and science fiction. There's something magical about how a good book can transport you to different worlds or introduce revolutionary ideas that reshape your thinking.

My journey is driven by an insatiable curiosity to explore the intersections of technology, human behavior, and innovation. I believe in building solutions that don't just work, but make a meaningful impact. Whether it's implementing neural networks on FPGAs, or writing about emerging technologies, I'm always pushing to understand the 'why' behind the 'how'.
